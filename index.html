How to Make Mac & Cheese for Journalists

How to make an amazing Mac & Cheese!

Assembly time: 30 minutes
Awesome rating: 97% awesome

Journalsits can't write on an empty stomach or conduct interviews while they're hangry, so here's how to make a fast and easy meal! Macaroni and Cheese is perfect for every day and perfect for every journalist.

  • Things You'll Need

  • 2lbs Macaroni (if you don't have Macaroni, any type of pasta is fine)
  • A pinch of salt
  • 2tbsp vegetable oil
  • 6tbsp butter
  • 8tbsp flour
  • 3 cups milk
  • 6 cups shredded chedar cheese

  1. Boil Water

    Fill a pot about 3/4ths of the way full of water, then set it on a stove burner until the water is boiling.

  2. Add the Pasta

    Cook your pasta for however long the packaging says, or until the pasta is al dente.

  3. Strain the Pasta

    Once the pasta is cooked, use a strainer to get rid of the water.

  4. Mix Butter and Oil

    In a new pot, mix the oil and butter until the butter melts into the oil.

  5. Add the Flour and Milk

    Stir in the flour until the sauce is smooth, then slowly stir in the milk.

  6. Add the Cheese.

    Finally, add the cheese and pour the sauce over your pasta.
